Crystal Collector

You will be given a random numbe at the start of the game. There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score. You win the game by matching your total score to the random number, you lose the gam if your total score goes above the random number. The value of each crystal is hidden from you until click on it. Each time the game starts, the crystals' values will change
